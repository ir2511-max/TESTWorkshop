Bain & Comité Colbert Report: Luxury Shoppers Are Adopting AI Faster Than Brands Can Adapt

The fifth annual Bain & Company/Comité Colbert 'Luxury and Technology' report found that 22% of luxury houses now rank AI among their top three corporate priorities, up from just 5% in 2024, while 82% of top-tier luxury spenders used an AI tool during their most recent purchase.
